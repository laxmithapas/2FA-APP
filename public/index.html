A quick word on online security.

Strong Passwords: Always use a mix of letters (upper and lower case), numbers, and symbols. Avoid using personal information.

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): You've just set it up! It adds a crucial second layer of security to your accounts.

Phishing Scams: Be wary of unsolicited emails or messages asking for personal information. Always verify the sender.

Secure Networks: Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions. Use a VPN if you must.
